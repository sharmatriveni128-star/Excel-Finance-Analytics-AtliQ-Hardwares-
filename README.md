# Excel-Finance-Analytics-AtliQ-Hardwares- 

## Project Overview  

This project presents a comprehensive Finance Analytics solution for AtliQ Hardwares, focusing on analyzing Profit & Loss (P&L) performance across multiple dimensions such as time, region, and market.  


The objective is to simulate a real-world business scenario and derive data-driven insights to support strategic decision-making.

---

## Business Objectives  

- Analyze financial performance across FY 2019–2021  

- Track key metrics: Net Sales, COGS, Gross Margin, and GM%  

- Perform Year-over-Year (YoY) comparisons  

- Identify high-performing and underperforming markets  

- Evaluate regional profitability trends

## Dashboard & Reports

### GM% by Sub-Zone (Quarterly Analysis)  

- Quarterly comparison of Gross Margin % across regions  

- Covers: ANZ, India, NA, NE, ROA, SE  

- Enables geographical performance analysis

### P&L Monthly Report  

- Monthly breakdown of financial performance across fiscal years  

- Helps identify seasonality and revenue trends

### Yearly P&L Summary  

- Consolidated view of overall business performance  

- Includes YoY growth comparison (2021 vs 2020)  

- Provides quick executive-level insights  



### Market-wise P&L Report  

- Country-level financial insights  

- Highlights top-performing and underperforming markets  


 
## Tools and Technologies  


| Microsoft Excel | Data analysis, report creation, and dashboard development |

| Pivot Tables | Summarizing large datasets and generating P&L reports |

| Power Pivot | Building data model and managing relationships between tables |

| DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) | Creating calculated measures such as Gross Margin and GM% |

| Data Cleaning | Preparing raw data by handling missing values, duplicates, and inconsistencies |

---

## Key Insights  



- Net Sales increased by approximately 200% from 2020 to 2021  

- Gross Margin increased in absolute terms, while GM% showed a slight decline  

- Markets such as USA and India are major revenue contributors  

- Regional analysis indicates variation in profitability across zones
